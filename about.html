Our Legacy & Excellence

Shaping Pharmacy professionals with a tradition of quality since 1992.

Established in 1992

The Niveditha College of Pharmacy was established by highly qualified and experienced Pharmacy Professionals under the auspices of Niveditha Education Trust, Chintamani. For over three decades, our major goal has remained unchanged: imparting Quality Education and uplifting the Pharmacy Profession.

Our campus features a sprawling building of its own, specifically designed for academic focus. We take immense pride in our well-equipped laboratories and modern classrooms that provide the ideal environment for pharmaceutical training.
